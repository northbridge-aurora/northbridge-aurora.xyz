Security

Northbridge Aurora takes security seriously. If you believe you have found a vulnerability in our platform or services, we encourage responsible disclosure.

Reporting a Vulnerability

Please send reports to: security@northbridge-aurora.xyz

Include a description of the issue, reproduction steps, and any relevant evidence. We aim to acknowledge reports within 48 hours.

Scope

Our disclosure policy covers the EggTrail platform, associated APIs, and northbridge-aurora.xyz infrastructure. For full details, see our security.txt.

Supply Chain Transparency

We publish SBOM summaries and build attestations for partner assurance. See our SBOM & Attestations FAQ for details.
